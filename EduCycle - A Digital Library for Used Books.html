Old Books, New Journey

Our platform connects students to share books, reduce costs, and promote sustainability through easy book exchange.

Key Features

1

User Registration/Login

Students can create profiles using their email or college ID.

2

Upload Books

Donors can upload details of books, including title, subject, author, semester, and photos.

3

Search & Filter

Students can search for books by subject, semester, course, or location.

4

Book Request/Reserve

Interested users can request or reserve books from donors.

5

Chat/Messaging System

Secure in-app messaging system for communication related to book exchange.

6

Donation or Selling

Users can offer books for free or at low prices.

7

Pickup/Drop Coordination

Options for coordinating meetings or courier services for book exchange.

8

Sustainability Score

Track how many books you've donated or reused to encourage eco-conscious behavior.
